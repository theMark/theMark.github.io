metpro — coaching platform

Designer & Developer MetPro Web & Mobile

A nutrition coaching platform across mobile and web — client app, coach console, and marketing site. Five years translating one-on-one coaching into software that clients use every day.

context

MetPro started as a high-touch personal-coaching service for nutrition and fitness. The challenge was preserving the quality of that one-on-one relationship while letting the business serve far more clients than its coaches could see individually.

That meant building software that felt like a coach in your pocket, not another generic tracking app.

my role

Designer and developer. Owned interaction and visual design, and shipped production code across the client mobile app, the coaching console, and the marketing site. Worked closely with the founding team, the coaching staff, and a small engineering group across five years of product evolution.

what I did

A few of the threads:

  • Designed and built the client mobile app — meal plans, meal and snack logging, weigh-ins, and a metabolic activity view that let clients see where their metabolism was at any given moment.
  • Designed and built the coaching console — where coaches log in, message their clients, and send personalized meal plans. React on the front, Ruby on Rails behind it.
  • Designed and built the marketing site in Ruby on Rails, including a metabolic survey funnel — a quiz that captured leads and routed prospects into a coaching trial.
  • Partnered with the coaching team across five years to translate their methodology into product surfaces that nudged behavior without nagging.
