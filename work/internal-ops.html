lightspeed — internal platform

Product Designer Freedom Forever Internal platform + voice AI

Lightspeed is Freedom Forever's internal platform for running a national residential solar operation — scheduling, crew management, the install pipeline, ERP databases, and timecards in one system used daily by hundreds of operators. Plus an AI-powered voice agent that handles customer support with live system monitoring during the call.

context

Behind every residential solar install is a long chain of office work — surveys, permits, scheduling, crew assignments, installs, inspections, commissioning, billing. Lightspeed is the platform that ties all of that together at Freedom Forever: one system where the calendar meets the pipeline meets the ERP, with data flowing through to payroll, customer support, and everything else the company runs on.

The platform serves hundreds of operators across roles — dispatchers, project managers, payroll, support. Each role has its own surface inside Lightspeed, but they're all looking at the same underlying truth: where every solar project is in its journey, and what needs to happen next.

my role

Product designer across multiple surfaces of Lightspeed. Owned design on the field calendar (scheduling), the ERP database tools, and timecard management. Contributed to the install pipeline. Plus designed and built the workflows behind the AI customer support voice agent — inside Freedom Forever's existing agent framework, not building the framework itself.

what I did

A few of the threads:

  • Designed the field calendar — the surface where dispatchers and project managers schedule and rebalance crews against jobs. Density was the hard problem: a national operation has thousands of jobs in flight at once, and the calendar had to make sense at a glance.
  • Designed the ERP database tools — the back-office interfaces for managing the custom data structures the rest of the business runs on. Operators in this space need precision and confidence, not speed.
  • Designed timecard management — making payroll inputs faster and harder to get wrong, against the messy reality of field crews logging time across jobs and locations.
  • Built the AI customer support voice agent — workflows in n8n orchestrating ElevenLabs (voice) and Claude Code, so the agent can check and monitor a customer's live solar system in real time during a call. Built inside Freedom Forever's existing agent framework.

outcome

Lightspeed is the day-to-day work environment for hundreds of operators across Freedom Forever — dispatchers, project managers, payroll, support — running a national install operation. The voice agent is in production handling customer support calls with live system monitoring. Mature, scaled, and still actively iterating.
