magined — an AI room designer, built solo

Snap a photo of a room, pick a style, and magined redesigns it in seconds — then makes everything in it shoppable. I built all of it myself: the app, the AI pipeline, and the business around it.

Role
Founder, designer & engineer
Timeline
2026 — ongoing
Platform
iOS app
Built with
Claude Code, Agent SDK, Figma, Nano Banana

01 What it is

Redecorating is a visual decision, but shopping for it isn't — you're left imagining how a piece looks in your space. magined closes that gap: see your room redesigned in any style, then buy what you like straight from the result.

02 Why it's here

I'm the founder, designer, and only engineer. The app, the image-generation pipeline, the shoppable gallery, and the agent backbone that keeps it running are all mine — built with Claude and an agent-first workflow.

It's the clearest proof of how I work now: one person carrying a product from idea to revenue. Same judgment I'd bring to your team, with the AI leverage already wired in.
