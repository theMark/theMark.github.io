Approach

How the work gets made.

Three principles that hold across twenty years and every product on this page.

01

Go where the work happens

Solar crews on rooftops, operators eight hours deep in scheduling tools, coaches juggling hundreds of clients — every product I've designed started with the real workflow, not the idealized one on the org chart. The best design decisions are found in the field, not the brief.

02

Build the real thing

A working product answers questions a clickable prototype can't. With Claude Code, the Agent SDK, and twenty years of front-end instincts, I go from Figma to production myself — which means faster iterations, honest feasibility checks, and designs that respect how software actually gets built.

03

Design for the ten-thousandth use

Most of my work is software people live in every day. Daily tools are won on speed, density, and muscle memory — the second week of use matters more than the first five minutes. Polish is what's left after you remove everything that slows someone down.
